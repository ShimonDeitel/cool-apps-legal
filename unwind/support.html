Unwind: Untangle Knots

Support

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Unwind gives you a hand-designed tangle to untangle by dragging the rope until every crossing is gone and it pulls taut. No timer, no score — just a peaceful, tactile puzzle.

Purchases

Unwind Pro is a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks three additional knot packs (15 more tangles). It is billed once through your Apple ID, not a subscription.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-left, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Unwind Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything the app tracks stays on your device. Unwind has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I reset my progress?
Open Settings from the gear icon and tap “Reset Progress.” You’ll be asked to confirm before anything is cleared.
My rope doesn't seem to respond — what's going on?
Drag directly on the rope itself, close to the point you want to move. If a puzzle looks stuck, you can back out to the pack list and reopen it to reset that tangle.
