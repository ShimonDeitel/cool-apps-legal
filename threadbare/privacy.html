Threadbare

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Threadbare (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. The app is private by default: it has no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs and no server of its own, and it does not sell your data.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

  • Photos you take or choose of clothing that needs mending.
  • The item name and repair type (button, hem, hole, zipper, or a custom label) you assign to each item.
  • The dates you logged and mended each item.
  • Whether you have subscribed to Threadbare Pro.

All of this stays on your device, saved in the app’s own private storage area. Photos are never uploaded to us, to any third party, or to any server — the app has no network connectivity of any kind beyond the App Store’s own purchase and subscription system.

Permissions we request

Threadbare asks for camera access so you can photograph an item that needs mending, and photo library access so you can choose an existing photo instead. You can decline either permission and still use the app by logging items without a photo. We never request location, contacts, microphone, or any other permission.

Where your data is stored

Your items and photos are stored on your device using standard iOS local storage. If your device is configured to use iCloud, your data may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity.

Purchases

The app offers an optional auto-renewable monthly subscription (“Threadbare Pro”, $2.99/month) that unlocks unlimited pending repairs, full mended history, and custom repair labels. Subscriptions renew automatically each month until cancelled, and are billed and managed entirely by Apple through your App Store account. We do not receive or store your payment card details. You can cancel anytime in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information. Because your content is stored on your device, you can exercise access and erasure directly: open the app to view your items, delete individual items from the item detail screen, or clear your mended archive from Settings. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual items at any time from the item detail screen, or clear your entire mended archive from Settings → Clear Mended Archive. To remove all of the app’s data, including every photo, delete the app; this permanently removes everything stored locally. If you sync through your own iCloud, manage or remove that data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
