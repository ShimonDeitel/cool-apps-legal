Threadbare helps you keep track of the small mending jobs scattered across your wardrobe. Photograph an item, tag what it needs (a button, a hem, a hole, a zipper, or something else), and mark it mended when you fix it — complete with a satisfying stitched-healing animation.

Purchases

Threadbare Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed at $2.99 per month through your Apple ID. It unlocks unlimited pending repairs (the free tier allows up to 5 active items at once), your full mended history (the free tier shows your most recent 10 mended items), and custom repair labels beyond the five built-in presets. It renews automatically each month until you cancel.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the settings icon in the top-right of the Pending screen, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your device, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions, find Threadbare, and cancel there.
Is my data private?
Yes. Every photo and item you log stays on your device. Threadbare has no network access beyond App Store purchases, and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
What happens if I hit the 5-item free limit?
You can still fully use the app — mark existing items as mended to free up a slot, or subscribe to Threadbare Pro for unlimited pending repairs.
How do I clear my mended archive?
Open Settings and tap “Clear Mended Archive.” You’ll be asked to confirm, since this permanently removes your mended history and photos.
