Bodymap helps you notice patterns in where discomfort shows up over time. Tap a spot on the body silhouette to log an entry with an intensity rating and an optional note. Over time, Bodymap aggregates your entries into a heatmap over the same silhouette, so clusters that are easy to miss day-to-day — like a shoulder or knee that keeps coming up — become visible.

Not a medical tool

Bodymap is a personal pattern journal, not a medical device or diagnostic tool. It does not interpret symptoms, diagnose conditions, or replace a conversation with a doctor. If you have ongoing pain or discomfort, please talk to a medical professional — Bodymap can help you describe what you have noticed, nothing more.

Purchases

Bodymap Pro is a one-time, non-consumable purchase ($2.99, paid once, not a subscription) that unlocks unlimited history, the back-view body silhouette, and data export. The free app fully solves the core problem on its own, forever: unlimited front-view logging, a 30-day heatmap and timeline, and the quiet-weeks streak.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, go to the Settings tab, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
Is Bodymap Pro a subscription?
No. It is a single one-time purchase. You pay once and it is yours for good — nothing renews or recurs.
What happens to my older entries on the free tier?
They are never deleted. Entries older than 30 days are simply hidden from view until you purchase or restore Bodymap Pro, at which point your full history becomes visible again.
What does the “quiet weeks” streak mean?
It counts the days since your last logged entry, framing stretches with nothing to report as worth noticing too — not just presence of symptoms.
How do I delete all my data?
Go to Settings and tap “Delete All Data.” You will be asked to confirm, since this cannot be undone.
Does Bodymap require an internet connection?
No. Bodymap works entirely offline. All your data stays on your device.
