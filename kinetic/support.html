Purchases

Kinetic Pro is a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks a custom color picker so you can draw in any color, not just the 5 free presets. It is billed once through your Apple ID, not a subscription.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, go to Settings, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Kinetic Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Kinetic has no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and it doesn't collect any personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I clear the sand and start over?
Use the clear/reset control in the main drawing view to flatten the surface back to a clean slate.
Can I adjust how the sand settles?
Yes — open Settings to adjust touch (brush) size and trail persistence, controlling how quickly your marks fade or how long they linger.
