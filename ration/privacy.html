Ration

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Ration (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. The app is private by default: it has no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, no network access, and it does not sell your data.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

  • The name of the item you are rationing (for example, “Bag of chips”) and how many portions you divided it into.
  • Which portions you have consumed and on what dates, used to lock future portions until the next day and to calculate your streak.
  • Your haptic feedback preference.
  • Whether you have an active Ration Pro subscription.
  • If you use Ration Pro’s custom photo feature, a photo you choose from your library to represent your item.

All of this stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to us or to any third party.

Permissions we request

Ration does not request access to your camera, location, contacts, or microphone. Ration Pro subscribers can optionally choose a photo from their photo library, using Apple’s PhotosPicker, to visually represent their rationed item. This picker runs in a separate, sandboxed process — the app only receives the single photo you explicitly select, and that photo is stored solely on your device. The app has no network connectivity of any kind for your item data or photos.

Where your data is stored

Your item data, consumption history, and any selected photo are stored on your device using standard iOS local storage. If your device is configured to use iCloud, your data may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity.

Subscriptions

The app offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (“Ration Pro”, $2.99/month) that unlocks tracking multiple items at once, custom item photos, and streak history. Subscriptions and billing are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information. Because your content is stored on your device, you can exercise access and erasure directly: open the app to view your item and history, and reset it within the app’s Settings, or by deleting the app. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

You can reset your current item at any time from Settings → Reset Current Item. To remove all of the app’s data from your device, delete the app; this removes the locally stored content, including any selected photo. If you sync through your own iCloud, manage or remove that data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
