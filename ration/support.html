Subscription

Ration Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed at $2.99/month through your Apple ID. It unlocks tracking multiple items at the same time, replacing the generic silhouette with your own photo of the item, and a streak history showing how many days in a row you stuck to your portion plan. You can cancel any time in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions; your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-left, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe to Ration Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your item, portion, and history data stay on your device. Ration has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
Why is my portion locked?
Ration allows one portion per calendar day so your item is stretched out over time. Once you consume today’s portion, the next one unlocks tomorrow.
How do I reset my current item?
Open Settings from the gear icon and tap “Reset Current Item.” You’ll be asked to confirm before your item and its history are cleared.
Can I track more than one item?
Yes, with Ration Pro. Free users can ration one item at a time; Pro subscribers can track multiple items — like chips, soda, and cookies — simultaneously.
