Uncoil

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Uncoil (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. The app is private by default: it has no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, no network access, and it does not sell your data. The cord physics simulation runs entirely on your device.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

  • Your haptic feedback preference.
  • Your chosen cord skin.
  • Which daily tangles you have solved (Pro daily archive).
  • Whether you have an active Uncoil Pro subscription.

All of this stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to us or to any third party.

Permissions we request

None. Uncoil does not request access to your camera, photo library, location, contacts, or any other protected resource. The app has no network connectivity of any kind — every tangle is generated and solved entirely on your device.

Where your data is stored

Your settings and archive are stored on your device using standard iOS local storage. If your device is configured to use iCloud, your data may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity.

Subscriptions

The app offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (“Uncoil Pro”, product ID uncoil_pro_monthly) billed at $2.99 per month. The subscription automatically renews each month unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings (Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your device). Purchases and billing are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information. Because your content is stored on your device, you can exercise access and erasure directly: open the app to view your settings and archive, and reset them within the app, or by deleting the app. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

To remove all of the app’s data from your device, delete the app; this removes the locally stored content. If you sync through your own iCloud, manage or remove that data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
