Subscription

Uncoil Pro is an auto-renewing subscription billed at $2.99 per month through your Apple ID. It unlocks additional cord skins (rope, string lights, garden hose), harder multi-knot tangles, and your daily tangle history/archive. It renews automatically each month unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel anytime in Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your device.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-left, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe to Uncoil Pro.
How do I cancel my subscription?
On your device, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions, select Uncoil, and tap Cancel Subscription. This can also be done from the App Store app under your account.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything the app tracks stays on your device. Uncoil has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
My cord doesn't seem to respond — what's going on?
Drag directly on the cord itself, close to the point you want to move. If a tangle looks stuck, tap “New Tangle” to generate a fresh one.
