Purchases

Refundable Pro is a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks unlimited tracked returns, warranty tracking, and full history. It is billed once through your Apple ID, not a subscription.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap the add button, and if prompted tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Refundable Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything you log stays on your device. The app has no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I delete my data?
Delete individual items inside the app. To remove everything from your device, delete the app. If you sync through your own iCloud account, also remove the data in your iCloud settings.
