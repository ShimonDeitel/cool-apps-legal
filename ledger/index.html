What's inside

Safe to Spend

One honest number, recalculated the moment you log a purchase — not a monthly average, not a guess.

Two-tap logging

Amount, category, optional note. That's the whole form. No receipts to photograph, no accounts to link.

Weekly pattern

A seven-day bar chart so you can see exactly which day derailed you — tap a day to see what you bought.

Daily pace

Ledger divides what's left by the days remaining, so "safe to spend" always means safe to spend today.

Fully private

No network access, no analytics, no ads. Every entry lives on your device. Nothing is ever uploaded.

The Ledger Book

Set your weekly budget, manage your membership, and export or clear your data — all from one settings screen.
