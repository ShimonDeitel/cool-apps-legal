Purchases

Ledger Pro is an auto-renewing monthly subscription ($4.99/month) that unlocks multiple budget envelopes, a monthly pattern view, CSV export, and custom accent-color themes. The free app fully solves the core budgeting problem on its own, forever.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the book icon in the top-right, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, find Ledger, and cancel. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything the app tracks stays on your device. Ledger has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How is “safe to spend” calculated?
It's your weekly budget minus what you've already logged this week, so you always know exactly what's left to spend without going over.
How do I change my weekly budget?
Open the book icon in the top-right corner and edit “Weekly Budget” at the top of the page.
