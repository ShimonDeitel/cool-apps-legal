What Refill is — and isn't

Refill is a manual supply counter and reminder tool. It is not a medical device, does not read prescription labels, and performs no automated pill recognition or OCR — every count is entered by you. Refill-date estimates are convenience projections, not medical guidance. Always follow your prescriber's or pharmacist's instructions.
