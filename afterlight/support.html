Subscription

Afterlight Pro is an auto-renewing monthly subscription ($3.99/month) that unlocks unlimited custom labels (the free plan includes 4 preset labels), the full weekly-pattern history beyond the last 7 days, and custom accent color themes. It renews automatically each month unless cancelled, and can be managed or cancelled any time from your Apple ID subscription settings.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the settings icon in the top-right, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything the app tracks stays on your device. Afterlight has no network access, does not use Screen Time or location tracking, and has no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I add a custom label?
Open Settings and use the “Your labels” section to add a new label. Free accounts can have up to 4 labels; Afterlight Pro removes that limit.
The day-bar handles won't drag — what's going on?
Drag directly on the vertical white divider between two segments, not on the segment itself (tapping a segment opens the label picker instead). If a divider seems stuck, close and reopen the app.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple. Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, choose Afterlight Pro, and tap Cancel Subscription.
