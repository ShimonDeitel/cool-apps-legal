Handoff

Support

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Handoff is a shared-task relay for two people living together. Each recurring task — dishes, trash, dog walk, whatever you add — has a single baton that one of you holds. Tap “Done” and the baton arcs across the screen to the other person, logging that you completed it and handing them the next round. There are no accounts: it’s the physical hand-off, on one shared device, between the two of you.

Subscription

Handoff Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed at $2.99/month through your Apple ID. It unlocks unlimited shared tasks (the free plan covers up to 3), fairness history beyond the last 7 days, and custom baton/accent colors. You can cancel any time in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions; your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-right, and tap “Upgrade” then “Restore Purchases,” or use “Restore Purchases” directly in Settings. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your tasks, names, and hand-off history stay on your device. Handoff has no accounts, no network access, and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How many tasks can I track for free?
Up to 3 shared tasks, with the full baton mechanic and 7 days of fairness history. Handoff Pro removes the task limit and extends history.
Do both people need the app installed?
No. Handoff is designed to be used on a single shared device that both people pass back and forth — there is no login or second device required.
How do I change the two names?
Open Settings from the gear icon and edit the Person A / Person B fields at the top.
