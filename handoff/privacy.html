Handoff

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Handoff (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. The app is private by default: it has no accounts, no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, no network access, and it does not sell your data.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

  • The names of the two people sharing the device (for example, “Alex” and “Sam”) that you type into Settings.
  • The list of shared tasks you create (for example, “Dishes”, “Trash”, “Dog Walk”) and which person currently holds the baton for each one.
  • A history of completed hand-offs, used to show the fairness view.
  • Whether you have an active Handoff Pro subscription.

All of this stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to us or to any third party. Handoff has no accounts and no sign-in of any kind — it is designed as a single shared-device app that two people pass back and forth.

Permissions we request

Handoff does not request access to your camera, photo library, location, contacts, or microphone. The app has no network connectivity of any kind for your task or history data.

Where your data is stored

Your task list, names, and hand-off history are stored on your device using standard iOS local storage. If your device is configured to use iCloud, your data may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity.

Subscriptions

The app offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (“Handoff Pro”, $2.99/month) that unlocks unlimited shared tasks, fairness history beyond 7 days, and custom baton/accent colors. Subscriptions and billing are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information. Because your content is stored on your device, you can exercise access and erasure directly: open the app to view your tasks and history, and delete tasks within the app or by deleting the app. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual shared tasks (and their history) from within the app at any time. To remove all of the app’s data from your device, delete the app; this removes the locally stored content. If you sync through your own iCloud, manage or remove that data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
