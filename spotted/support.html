Spotted

Support

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Spotted keeps track of whose car is in which household parking spot. Define your spots (Driveway Left, Garage, Street, and so on) and your household’s cars, then tap a spot on the board to claim it for a car or mark it empty. Every change is logged so you can see when each car last moved.

Not a tracking feature

The “Who’s Home” summary on the board is a simple inference based on which cars are currently assigned to a spot — it reflects your last manual update, not live GPS or location tracking. Spotted has no location permissions and no network connectivity.

Purchases

Spotted Pro is a one-time, non-consumable purchase ($2.99, paid once, not a subscription) that unlocks unlimited spots and cars, full move history, and custom color themes. The free app fully solves the core problem on its own, forever: up to 3 spots, 3 cars, the complete tap-to-claim board, and 7 days of history.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, go to the Settings tab, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
Is Spotted Pro a subscription?
No. It is a single one-time purchase. You pay once and it is yours for good — nothing renews or recurs.
What happens to my older history on the free tier?
It is never deleted. Entries older than 7 days are simply hidden from view until you purchase or restore Spotted Pro, at which point your full history becomes visible again.
How do guest cars work?
Tap a spot, then “Add Guest Car” to quickly claim it for a visitor without adding them as a permanent household car. Guest cars are automatically cleared from the roster once they are no longer parked anywhere.
How do I delete all my data?
Go to Settings and tap “Delete All Data.” You will be asked to confirm, since this cannot be undone.
Does Spotted require an internet connection?
No. Spotted works entirely offline. All your data stays on your device.
