Spotted

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Spotted (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. Spotted is a household parking-spot board that works entirely on your device: your data never leaves your phone. There is no server, no account, no analytics SDK, and no network call anywhere in this app.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

  • The parking spots you define (names such as “Driveway Left” or “Garage”).
  • The household cars and guest cars you add, including the name and color you choose for each.
  • Which car is currently assigned to which spot, and a log of past spot changes (the history).
  • Your haptics preference and whether you have purchased Spotted Pro.

All of this stays on your device, inside the app’s own private storage folder. Nothing is uploaded to us or to any third party, ever.

Not a location or tracking feature

Spotted does not use GPS, Bluetooth, or any form of live location tracking. The “Who’s Home” summary shown in the app is purely a derived inference based on the spot assignments you manually entered — it reflects your last update, not your family’s real-time location. Spotted has no way to know where anyone actually is.

Where your data is stored

Your spots, cars, and history are stored on your device using standard iOS local file storage (your app’s own Application Support directory). If your device is configured to use iCloud device backup, your data may be backed up through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your data, and we cannot access it.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity, and we could not share your data with anyone even if we wanted to — the app has no network connectivity of any kind.

Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time, non-consumable purchase (“Spotted Pro,” $2.99, purchased once) that unlocks unlimited spots and cars, full move history, and custom color themes. This is a single purchase, not a subscription — it does not renew or recur, and it is billed once through the App Store. Purchase processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card details, and Pro access is tied to your Apple ID so you can restore it on any device using “Restore Purchases.”

Free-tier users can manage up to 3 spots and 3 household cars, use the full tap-to-claim board mechanic, and see up to 7 days of move history — forever, with no time limit. Older history entries are never deleted — they remain safely stored on-device and become visible again as soon as Pro is purchased or restored.

Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA/CPRA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information — we have no way to access it in the first place. Because your content is stored on your device, you can exercise access and erasure directly: open the app to view your spots and cars, use “Delete All Data” in Settings, or delete the app entirely. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

To remove all of the app’s data from your device, use the “Delete All Data” destructive action in Settings (which requires confirmation), or delete the app itself; either removes all locally stored spots, cars, and history. If you sync through your own iCloud device backup, manage or remove that data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
