What Plumb is

Plumb is a personal photo check-in and manual measurement tool. It lets you take a daily photo of yourself, draw your own straight reference line (a “plumb line”) over that photo, and compare photos from different dates side by side. Plumb is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and does not perform automated posture detection, diagnosis, or analysis of any kind.
