Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-left, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe to Overflow Pro.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple. On your iPhone, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions, find Overflow, and cancel there.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything the app tracks stays on your device. Overflow has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
What happens when my glass overflows?
You'll see a simple, wordless full-screen splash. Tap anywhere to pour it out and reset for the rest of the day.
Does my glass reset automatically?
Yes. Every glass resets once at the start of a new calendar day, whether or not you have the app open at midnight.
How do I reset today's count manually?
Open Settings from the gear icon and tap “Reset Today.” You’ll be asked to confirm before anything is cleared.
