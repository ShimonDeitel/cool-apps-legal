Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-right, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
Is Wallet Count Pro a subscription?
No. It is a single one-time purchase. You pay once and it is yours for good — nothing renews or recurs.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything the app tracks stays on your device. Wallet Count has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How is “should have” calculated?
It's your starting cash amount minus every spend you've logged in the current period, so you always know exactly what should be left in your wallet.
What does the reconciliation feature do?
Tap “Reconcile” on the home screen, count your physical cash, and enter what you actually have. The app shows the honest gap between what it expected and what you counted — it never silently corrects your total.
How many wallets can I have?
The free tier includes one wallet. Wallet Count Pro unlocks multiple simultaneous wallets, for example one for groceries cash and one for personal spending cash.
