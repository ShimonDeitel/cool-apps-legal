Why it's different

Built around one honest question: what's actually left?

No bank linking, no budgeting categories to fight with. Just the cash you started with, minus what you've spent — rendered as bills you can watch disappear.

Visual bill-stack

Start a period with your real denominations — $1s, $5s, $20s, $100s. Wallet Count renders them as a physical stack, color-coded exactly like the paper in your hand.

Bills peel away, not a ticker

Log a spend and the top of the stack physically slides off and fades — a satisfying, tactile depletion animation instead of a boring number ticking down.

Reconcile against real bills

Count the physical cash left in your wallet and compare it to what the app expects. Instantly see if you're short, and by how much.

Full spend history

Every logged spend and reconciliation lives in a clean history view, so a night out or a week of cash use is easy to look back on.

Multiple wallets (Pro)

Track a personal cash wallet and a separate tip jar or business float side by side, each with its own color theme.

100% private, on-device

No bank login, no network access at all. Every wallet, spend, and reconciliation lives only on your phone.
