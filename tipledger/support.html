Purchases

Tip Ledger Pro is a one-time, non-consumable purchase ($2.99, paid once, not a subscription) that unlocks multi-year history, custom tip percentage presets, and CSV/text export of your tip log. The free app fully solves the core problem on its own, forever: unlimited tip calculations, full current-year tracking, and a complete category and monthly breakdown.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, go to the Settings tab, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
Is Tip Ledger Pro a subscription?
No. It is a single one-time purchase. You pay once and it is yours for good — nothing renews or recurs.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything the app tracks stays on your device. Tip Ledger has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
What happens to my tips at the start of a new year?
Each year's total starts fresh on January 1. The free tier only keeps the current year visible; upgrading to Pro keeps every past year archived so you can compare year over year.
What is the “tipping personality” feature?
It's a simple, non-judgmental label based on your average tip percentage this year — purely descriptive and never guilt-inducing, just a fun way to see your own pattern.
How many tip entries can I log?
Unlimited, on both the free tier and Pro. There is no cap on the number of tips you can track in the current year.
