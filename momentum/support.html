Purchases

Momentum Pro is a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks four additional shape materials — glass, wood, neon, and gold — each with its own weight, bounce, and friction. It is billed once through your Apple ID, not a subscription.

Frequently asked questions

How do I add a shape?
Tap anywhere on the empty scene to drop a new shape of your currently selected material. Drag any shape and release to flick it with real momentum.
How do I switch materials?
Use the material picker at the bottom of the screen. Locked materials show a lock icon — tap one to see what Momentum Pro unlocks.
How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap the gear icon, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Momentum Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Momentum has no camera, photo, location, or network access at all. Everything runs entirely on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I change gravity or turn off haptics?
Open Settings (gear icon) to adjust gravity strength or toggle haptic feedback on collisions.
