Momentum

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Momentum (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. The app is private by default: it has no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, no network access, and it does not sell your data.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

  • Your chosen shape material and gravity/haptics preferences.

All of this stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to us or to any third party. Momentum has no camera, photo library, location, or network permissions of any kind — it is a purely on-device physics toy.

Permissions we request and why

None. Momentum does not request camera, photo library, location, contacts, or any other system permission.

Where your data is stored

Your preferences are stored locally on your device. If your device is configured to use iCloud, your data may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. The app makes no network requests of any kind.

Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time in-app purchase (“Momentum Pro”) that unlocks four additional shape materials (glass, wood, neon, gold). Purchases and billing are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information. Because your preferences are stored on your device, you can exercise access and erasure directly: open the app to view your settings, and reset them within the app or by deleting the app. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

To remove all of the app’s data from your device, delete the app; this removes the locally stored preferences. If you sync through your own iCloud, manage or remove that data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and post the revised version here.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email s0533495227@gmail.com.
