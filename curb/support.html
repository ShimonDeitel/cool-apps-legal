Purchases

Curb Pro is a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks meter alerts, photo notes, and full parking history. It is billed once through your Apple ID, not a subscription.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap “Pro,” and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Curb Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your location and photos stay on your device. The app has no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and your location is never sent to us. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I delete my data?
Clear your current spot or history inside the app. To remove everything from your device, delete the app. If you sync through your own iCloud account, also remove the data in your iCloud settings.
