Purchases

Spot Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed at $2.99/month through your Apple ID. It unlocks unlimited spot history (beyond the free 5), search and filter by date, custom text labels per spot, and room-based tagging. It renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Frequently asked questions

How do I take a spot photo?
Open Spot and tap the large shutter button on the home screen. It takes an instant photo and adds it to your stack.
How do I see my recent spots?
Shake your phone. Your last spots fan out in a card stack, newest on top, and you can swipe through them.
How many photos does the free version keep?
The free version always keeps your last 5 spots. You can tap the shutter as many times as you like — only the newest 5 are kept unless you upgrade to Spot Pro.
How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-left, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, select Spot, and tap Cancel Subscription.
Is my data private?
Yes. Every photo you take with Spot stays on your device. Spot has no network access and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I delete all my photos?
Open Settings from the gear icon and tap “Clear All Photos.” You’ll be asked to confirm before anything is deleted.
