Spot

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Spot (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. Spot is private by default: it has no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, no network access, and it does not sell your data.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

  • Photos you take with Spot of where you just set something down.
  • The timestamp of each photo.
  • If you are a Spot Pro subscriber: optional text labels and room tags you add to a photo.
  • Whether you have an active Spot Pro subscription.

Your photos never leave your device. Spot has no network access of any kind — nothing is uploaded to us, to any third party, or to any cloud service that we operate.

Camera access

Spot requests camera access solely so you can take an instant photo of where you set an item down. The camera is only used when you tap the shutter button; Spot does not access the camera in the background and does not record video.

Where your data is stored

Every photo and its timestamp are stored on your device using standard iOS local storage (the app’s own Documents directory). If your device is configured to use iCloud, your data may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your photos or any other data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity.

Purchases

The app offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (“Spot Pro”, $2.99/month) that unlocks unlimited photo history, search and filter by date, custom text labels, and room-based tagging. Purchases and billing are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, and can be managed or cancelled at any time in your Apple ID account settings.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information. Because all of your photos are stored on your device, you can exercise access and erasure directly: open the app to view your spots, and delete them within the app’s Settings using “Clear All Photos,” or by deleting the app. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

You can delete all of your photos at any time from Settings → Clear All Photos, which is confirmed before anything is removed. To remove all of the app’s data from your device, delete the app; this removes every locally stored photo. If you sync through your own iCloud, manage or remove that data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
