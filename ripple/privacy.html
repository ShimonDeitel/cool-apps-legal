Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Ripple (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. The app is private by default: it has no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and it does not sell your data.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

What data the app handles

Ripple does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app state — your chosen colors, shapes, and Pro unlock status — is stored only on your device using standard iOS local storage. Nothing is sent to us or to any third party.

Permissions we request and why

Ripple does not request any device permissions. It does not use your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library.

Where your data is stored

Your preferences and Pro unlock status are stored on your device. If your device is configured to use iCloud, this data may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores any app data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles, and no data about your use of the app is ever sent to us.

Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time in-app purchase (“Ripple Pro”) that unlocks a custom color picker and extra shapes. Purchases and billing are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 — indeed, we do not collect personal information from anyone. If you have a concern, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information, and because the app collects none, there is nothing on our servers to access or erase. Because your content is stored only on your device, you can remove it at any time by deleting the app. For any other request, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

Delete the app to remove all locally stored preferences and Pro status from your device. If you sync through your own iCloud account, also remove the data in your iCloud settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to s0533495227@gmail.com.
