Purchases

Ripple Pro is a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks a custom color picker and extra shapes. It is billed once through your Apple ID, not a subscription.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap “Pro,” and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Ripple Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Ripple does not collect any data at all. Everything runs entirely on your device with no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I delete my data?
Delete the app to remove all locally stored preferences and Pro status from your device.
