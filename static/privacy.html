Static: Calm the Room

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Static: Calm the Room (“the app”, “we”) does with your information. The app is private by default: it has no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and the one sensor it uses — your microphone — is measured only in real time, on your device.

Who we are

The data controller for this app is Shimon Deitel (Joshua Deitel). You can reach us at s0533495227@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

Microphone use — never recorded, never uploaded

Static uses your device’s microphone solely to measure the instantaneous loudness of the room (via on-device audio metering) so the storm cloud animation can visibly calm down as things get quieter. This is a live measurement only: the app never records, saves, stores, or transmits any audio, anywhere, at any time. No audio file is ever created and no audio data ever leaves your device. You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings, and the app will simply stop reacting to sound.

What data the app handles

  • A live, transient decibel reading used only to drive the on-screen animation.
  • Your chosen visual theme and accessibility preferences (e.g. whether to show the numeric dB reading).
  • For Pro subscribers, a short in-session history of noise levels (kept in memory for the current session, used to draw the history graph and quiet-streak tracker) and whether you have an active Static Pro subscription.

All of this stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to us or to any third party.

Permissions we request

Static requests microphone access to measure ambient noise level in real time, as described above. It does not request access to your camera, photo library, location, contacts, or any other protected resource. The app has no network connectivity requirement of any kind for its core functionality.

Where your data is stored

Your preferences are stored on your device using standard iOS local storage. If your device is configured to use iCloud, this may be backed up or synced through your own Apple iCloud account, which is controlled by you and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We do not operate a server that stores your data.

No tracking or advertising

The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, and it does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity.

Subscription

The app offers an optional auto-renewing monthly subscription (“Static Pro”, $2.99/month) that unlocks additional calming visual themes, a session history graph, and a quiet-streak tracker. Purchases and billing are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store; the subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, and can be managed or cancelled in your Apple ID account settings. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will address it.

Your rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, and the right not to have your personal information sold. We do not sell personal information. Because your preferences and any in-session history are stored on your device (and the in-session history is never persisted beyond the current session), you can exercise access and erasure directly by closing the app or deleting it. For any request you cannot complete yourself, email us at s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond.

Deleting your data

To remove all of the app’s data from your device, delete the app; this removes locally stored preferences. Session history is never written to disk in the first place and disappears automatically when the app closes.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
