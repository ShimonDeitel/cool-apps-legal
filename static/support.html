Subscription

Static Pro is an auto-renewing monthly subscription ($2.99/month) that unlocks three additional calming themes (Still Pond, Forest, Night Sky), a session history graph of noise over time, and a quiet-streak tracker for naptime or study sessions. It renews automatically each month until cancelled.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?
Open the app, tap the gear icon in the top-left, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple. Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, choose Static Pro, and cancel there.
Is my microphone audio ever recorded or uploaded?
No. Static measures the loudness of the room in real time using on-device audio metering only. No audio is ever recorded to a file, stored, or sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for details.
The cloud doesn't seem to react to sound — what's going on?
Make sure you granted microphone access when first prompted. If you denied it, go to iOS Settings → Static → toggle Microphone on, then relaunch the app.
Can I see the actual decibel number?
Yes, if you want it. Open Settings from the gear icon and turn on “Show numeric dB reading.” It's off by default because the whole point of Static is a wordless, at-a-glance visual.
