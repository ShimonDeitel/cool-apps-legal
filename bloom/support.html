Bloom Pro is a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks four additional flower species (rose, tulip, lotus, and cherry blossom) alongside the free daisy. It is billed once through your Apple ID, not a subscription.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap the gear icon for Settings, and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Bloom Pro.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything you grow stays on your device. The app has no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs and makes no network requests. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I delete my data?
Open Settings and tap “Clear My Garden” to remove all saved blooms. To remove everything from your device, delete the app. If you sync through your own iCloud account, also remove the data in your iCloud settings.
Why don't I hear haptic feedback when I bloom a flower?
Check that Haptic Feedback is enabled in Settings, and that your device isn't in a low-power or silent-haptics mode.
